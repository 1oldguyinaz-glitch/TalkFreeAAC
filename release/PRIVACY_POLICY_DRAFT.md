# TalkFreeAAC Privacy Policy Draft

TalkFreeAAC is designed as a local-first AAC communication app.

## Data stored locally
The app may store:
- Communication history
- Completed sentences
- Word usage counts
- Favorites
- Recent words
- Emergency profile information
- Parent/team notes if enabled
- Display and accessibility settings

## Cloud storage
TalkFreeAAC does not require cloud storage for AAC communication.

Optional future features may allow parent-approved backup or team collaboration. These features should be opt-in and clearly labeled.

## Children
The child-facing communication interface does not expose settings, analytics, team tools, or administrative controls.

## Emergency data
Emergency profile information is controlled by the parent/admin and may include medical or contact information for safety use.

## Exports
Parents may export local backups. Exported files are controlled by the user after download.
