# TalkFreeAAC V4 Patch 02 — Language Engine Expansion

## Adds
- Human-first communication genome layer
- Favorite phrase engine
- Conversation prediction engine
- Life boards for common situations
- Improved search for phrases, aliases, topics, and communication purpose
- Topic navigation expansion
- Phrase usage tracking

## Fixes
- Multi-word phrases are now handled as phrases instead of awkward single-word taps
- "I love you" and other relationship phrases get priority
- Search for "love" returns "I love you"
- Bedtime, school, doctor, restaurant, playground, and emergency boards are available
