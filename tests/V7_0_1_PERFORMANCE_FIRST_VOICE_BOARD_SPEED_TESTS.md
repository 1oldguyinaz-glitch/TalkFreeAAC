# v7.0.1 Manual Tests

## Board speed

1. Open the child AAC board.
2. Tap `I`.
3. Confirm the next board populates immediately.
4. Tap `am` or `I'm`.
5. Confirm the next words/buckets populate without speech delay.

## Speech behavior

1. Confirm `Speak each word on tap` is off by default.
2. Tap several words: `I`, `am`, `hungry`.
3. Confirm the app does not speak each individual tap.
4. Tap Speak.
5. Confirm the app speaks the full sentence: `I am hungry`.

## Optional word tap speech

1. Go to Parent Dashboard → Voice Cadence.
2. Enable `Speak each word on tap`.
3. Return to the board.
4. Tap words.
5. Confirm each tapped speakable word is spoken.
6. Turn the setting off and confirm taps are silent again until Speak is pressed.

## Voice settings

1. Confirm Voice Type only shows six choices.
2. Confirm Cadence only shows three choices.
3. Confirm no long device/accent list is exposed.
4. Preview voice works.
